FANDOM * AFTER HOURS * CUSTOM

Saturday July 1st, 2017 we designed, produced and hosted a 21+ party across the street from Anime Expo 2017 on the rooftop of Don Chente. The night included performances by Moe Shop (making his United States debut), Hentai Dude (also known as Waifu Wednesdays), Tanuki and Akira Complex, Shiki, and W.T. Snacks.
