Operations

Hyuga comes to Power Level Productions with a diverse set of skills and strategic and tactical expertise in team management, Japanese business, and event production. Previously Operations Director at Azix, Inc. in New York City, he oversaw operations for Japan Week 2016 in Grand Central Terminal, Waku Waku +NYC 2015, the Japan Pavilion at the International Restaurant and Foodservice Show, Western Food Expo, and Florida Restaurant and Lodging Show.

During Japan Week, he was responsible for communicating and managing expectations for over 40 Japanese cultural and business partners. A member of the Hard Drop Tetris Community, NYC YoYo Club, and Kendama NYC, as well as an avid gamer and musician, Hyuga enjoys promoting fandom in all its forms.

He is also involved with the Super Smash Bros Melee community and co-founded a bi-weekly tournament with Alex “Captain Smuckers” Cottrell called Smash Corner, based in Scarsdale, New York. In his spare time he can be found volunteering as the Los Angeles regional coordinator for the Sukyo Mahikari Youth Group or at Brazilian Top Team Norwalk practicing martial arts.
